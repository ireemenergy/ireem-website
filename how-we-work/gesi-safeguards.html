GESI & Safeguards

Integrating gender equality, social inclusion, and safeguards across all our work.

Our Commitment

IREEM is committed to ensuring that our work promotes gender equality, social inclusion, and environmental responsibility.

We recognize that the benefits of sustainable development must reach all segments of society, with particular attention to women, persons with disabilities, and other marginalized groups.

GESI is not a separate workstream—it is a cross-cutting approach integrated into how we design, implement, and evaluate all our projects.

What is GESI?

Gender Equality: Ensuring equal opportunities and outcomes for all genders.

Disability Inclusion: Removing barriers and ensuring accessibility.

Social Inclusion: Engaging marginalized groups in decision-making and benefits.

How We Integrate GESI

Analysis

We conduct gender and inclusion analysis at project design stage to understand barriers and opportunities.

Participation

We ensure meaningful participation of women and marginalized groups in consultations and decision-making.

Benefits

We design activities to ensure equitable access to benefits including training, resources, and opportunities.

Environmental & Social Safeguards

Environmental Safeguards

  • • Environmental screening and assessment
  • • Impact mitigation measures
  • • Compliance with national regulations
  • • Monitoring and reporting

Social Safeguards

  • • Stakeholder engagement plans
  • • Grievance redress mechanisms
  • • Community consultation
  • • Labor and working conditions
