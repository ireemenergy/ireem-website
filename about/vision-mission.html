Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our work toward sustainable development.

Our Vision

"To become a leading institution in providing solutions for the management of natural resources, energy, and the environment based on science and technology that is innovative, integrative, and synergistic for sustainable development."

Our Mission

Three interconnected commitments that guide our daily work and long-term strategy.

01

Research & Technical Excellence

Delivering policy studies, technical assessments, energy audits, and environmental studies that meet the highest standards of scientific rigor and practical relevance.

  • • Policy research and analysis
  • • Technical assessments
  • • Energy audits
  • • Environmental impact studies
02

Knowledge Dissemination & Capacity Building

Transferring knowledge and building capabilities among stakeholders through training programs, technical assistance, and knowledge products.

  • • Professional training programs
  • • Certification support
  • • Technical assistance
  • • Guidelines and knowledge products
03

Collaborative Partnerships

Building and maintaining productive partnerships with domestic and international stakeholders to amplify impact and drive systemic change.

  • • Government collaboration
  • • International development partners
  • • Academic institutions
  • • Private sector engagement
