Partnership Models

Flexible collaboration options to match your objectives and requirements.

Donor-Funded Projects

We implement and coordinate donor-funded programs, managing complex multi-stakeholder initiatives from design through delivery.

Typical Activities:
  • • Project implementation and coordination
  • • Technical assistance delivery
  • • Capacity building programs
  • • Monitoring, evaluation, and reporting

Government Collaboration

We support government agencies with policy development, strategic planning, and technical assistance to strengthen institutional capacity.

Typical Activities:
  • • Policy research and recommendations
  • • Strategic plan development
  • • Regulatory framework support
  • • Capacity building for officials

Private Sector Engagement

We help businesses improve sustainability performance through audits, advisory services, and compliance support.

Typical Activities:
  • • Energy audits and efficiency advisory
  • • Environmental compliance support
  • • Sustainability strategy development
  • • Pilot project implementation

Research Partnerships

We collaborate with universities and research institutions on joint research, knowledge products, and academic exchanges.

Typical Activities:
  • • Joint research projects
  • • Knowledge product development
  • • Expert exchanges and seminars
  • • Student internship programs
