What We Do

Evidence-based solutions across three interconnected pillars for Indonesia's sustainable development.

Our Focus Areas

We deliver integrated solutions that address the complex challenges facing Indonesia's natural resources, energy, and environmental sectors.

Energy Transition & Low Carbon Development

Supporting Indonesia's transition to a sustainable energy future through policy advisory, capacity building, and implementation support for energy efficiency and renewable energy initiatives.

  • • Energy efficiency programs
  • • Renewable energy development
  • • Low-carbon development planning
  • • Energy modelling and analysis
Explore This Pillar

Natural Resources Management

Promoting sustainable management of Indonesia's natural resources through research, policy development, and stakeholder engagement to ensure long-term environmental and economic security.

  • • Resource baseline assessments
  • • Sustainable management frameworks
  • • Community-based management
  • • Natural capital accounting
Explore This Pillar

Environmental Management

Developing robust environmental monitoring, reporting, and verification systems while strengthening environmental governance and compliance across sectors.

  • • MRV system development
  • • Environmental compliance
  • • Climate change mitigation
  • • Environmental impact assessment
Explore This Pillar
