Natural Resources Management

Sustainable use and conservation of Indonesia's natural resources for current and future generations.

Why This Matters

Indonesia is one of the world's most biodiverse nations. These resources are fundamental to the nation's economy and livelihoods of millions.

However, unsustainable extraction and inadequate governance threaten these resources. Without effective management, Indonesia risks degrading its natural capital.

IREEM's Role

Research & Studies

Baseline assessments, ecosystem services valuation, resource mapping and sustainability assessments.

Policy Advisory

Policy analysis, regulatory framework development, spatial planning and governance strengthening.

Capacity Building

Stakeholder training, institutional strengthening, community-based management support.

Implementation

Pilot projects, community resource management, sustainable livelihood programs.
