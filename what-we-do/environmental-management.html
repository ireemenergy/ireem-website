Environmental Management

Developing robust environmental monitoring, reporting, and verification systems for Indonesia's climate commitments.

Why This Matters

Indonesia has committed to ambitious climate targets, including a 31.89% reduction in emissions by 2030. Achieving these goals requires robust systems to track, verify, and report environmental performance.

Effective environmental management is crucial for accessing international climate finance, meeting donor requirements, and demonstrating Indonesia's progress on its Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs).

IREEM's Role
